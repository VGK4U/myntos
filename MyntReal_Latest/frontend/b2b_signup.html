Frequently asked

You probably want to know…

Do I need a credit card for the trial?

No. Sign up with your work email and we’ll provision your tenant for 14 days, free. You can decide on a plan only after you’ve seen it run.

How does pro-rata billing work?

When you add or remove a module mid-cycle, we issue a single adjustment invoice for the remaining days of the current period. Per-seat modules multiply by your staff seat count automatically.

Can I run multiple legal entities?

Yes. Each tenant can have multiple GST-registered legal entities under one umbrella, with a default issuing entity for invoices. Tally/Zoho parity is built in.

What happens to my data if I leave?

Your data is yours. We provide full data exports on request. Your tenant is archived, not deleted, when you cancel — restore it whenever you want.

Do you support multi-currency?

Yes. Pick INR or USD per subscription. Module pricing is dual-currency, with per-client overrides if you need them.

Is my access to features tracked?

Every B2B mutation — module change, plan edit, subscription update, invoice issue — lands in the audit log with actor, before / after and timestamp.
