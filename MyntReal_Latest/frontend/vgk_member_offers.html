What is a Channel Partner?

A Channel Partner is an individual who acts as a bridge between potential customers and VGK4U's certified execution network. You identify genuine leads in your community — people interested in Solar panels, EV purchases, or Insurance products — and connect them through your personal VGK4U partner portal. Once the project is completed by our verified team, commission is credited directly to your wallet.

There is no selling, no installation, no technical work on your part. Your role is purely to connect and refer. Anyone with a smartphone and a network can participate.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers about how VGK4U works, what you earn, and what to expect.

01What is the value of 1 Point?
Each Point equals ₹1 in platform wallet value, credited to your account on joining. Points are tracked and used within the VGK4U partner platform.
02How many points do I get on joining?
10,000 Points on direct registration. An extra +5,000 Points if you join through a referral link — giving you up to 15,000 Points total.
03How do I earn actual income?
You earn commission when a customer you connect results in a completed Solar, EV, or Insurance project. Commission is calculated at published rates after platform service charge (8%) and Government TDS (2%).
04What deductions apply on payout?
8% platform service charge and 2% Government TDS are deducted at payout. Displayed commission rates are gross figures. These deductions ensure end-to-end execution, operational support, and legal compliance.
05Is registration free?
Yes. Registration is completely free. You start earning as a Registered Partner immediately — earning L1 and L5 commission on completed projects.
06Do I need the ₹5,000 Activation Package?
No — activation is optional. Free registration lets you earn L1 and L5 commissions. Activating with ₹5,000 unlocks all 5 commission levels, gives you 50,000 additional points, and unlocks the Points Auto-Refill benefit.
07Is income guaranteed?
No. Income depends entirely on your activity, genuine customer connections, and successful project completions. No project completion = No commission.
08Can I track my leads and earnings?
Yes. You get access to the VGK4U partner dashboard with live tracking for leads, project progress, commission status, and your points balance.
09Is this an MLM or network scheme?
No. VGK4U earnings come from real completed projects and verified services — not from recruitment or building downlines. The multi-level commission structure rewards your network's project activity, not head count.
10Can I do this part-time?
Yes. VGK4U is a flexible model. You can start part-time alongside your existing work and scale based on your results — there are no minimum targets.
11What does "Lifetime Income" mean?
Lifetime income is paid as long as you remain active — the conditions differ by category:

🏪 Showroom Setup (EV B2B): Lifetime eligibility requires a minimum of 5 bikes billed every 2 months. Falling below this resets the lifetime status for that period.

☀️ Solar & 🛵 Vehicle Sales (EV B2C): Lifetime = as long as you remain active and participate in all team meetings and support activities.

⚠️ Inactive Definition: A partner is considered inactive if there is no participation in team support or meetings for 60 consecutive days. Inactive partners will not receive further lifetime commission until re-activation.
