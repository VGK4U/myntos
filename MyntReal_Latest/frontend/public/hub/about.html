Who We Are

Mynt Real LLP is a registered multi-service business entity based in Andhra Pradesh, operating across electric vehicle mobility, solar energy, insurance facilitation, real estate services, and professional EV training.

The company operates primarily through the Royal Manthra EV and Har Ghar Solar brands, serving customers across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana with structured service delivery and partner-led distribution.

The VGK4U ecosystem represents the company's partner network — a structured program connecting independent business partners with MyntReal's products and services across the region.
