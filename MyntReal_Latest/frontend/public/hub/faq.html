Ecosystem Structure
VGK4U & the Partner Network
Understanding how MyntReal and VGK4U relate to each other.
What is VGK4U? +

VGK4U is the partner ecosystem of MyntReal. It is a structured program connecting independent business partners with MyntReal's products and services — primarily Royal Manthra EV 2-wheelers and Har Ghar Solar installations — across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Partners in the VGK4U network operate under the MyntReal umbrella, accessing shared products, service infrastructure, and operational support.

How is VGK4U different from MyntReal? +

MyntReal (Mynt Real LLP) is the registered legal entity — the company that holds MCA registration, GSTIN, ISO certification, and operates the business verticals.

VGK4U is the partner and member network that operates under MyntReal — the ecosystem connecting individual partners to the company's products, services, and distribution infrastructure.

What does a VGK4U partner do? +

A VGK4U partner operates as an independent business partner within the MyntReal ecosystem. This involves:

• Facilitating sales of Royal Manthra EV 2-wheelers and Har Ghar Solar installations in their local area

• Assisting customers with insurance facilitation through VGK Care

• Supporting real estate service referrals through VGK Real Dreams

Partners earn based on documented product/service transactions within the ecosystem.

Why does MyntReal operate across multiple verticals? +

The multi-vertical structure reflects a deliberate design: EV mobility and solar energy are the primary business focus, with insurance and real estate serving as complementary financial protection and asset categories for the same customer base.

A customer purchasing a Royal Manthra EV may also need vehicle insurance (VGK Care) or is evaluating property investment (VGK Real Dreams) — the ecosystem serves overlapping customer needs across related categories.

Products & Services
Royal Manthra EV, Solar & More
What MyntReal actually sells and facilitates.
What is Royal Manthra EV? +

Royal Manthra EV is MyntReal's primary electric two-wheeler brand. The vehicles use LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) battery technology and are sold and serviced through MyntReal's partner network in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

For detailed specifications and pricing, visit the Manthra EV page.

What is Har Ghar Solar? +

Har Ghar Solar is MyntReal's solar energy facilitation vertical. It helps customers access the Government of India's PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana — a subsidised rooftop solar scheme offering up to ₹78,000 in subsidies.

MyntReal facilitates the subsidy application process, DISCOM coordination, and connects customers with installation partners. For more details, visit the Har Ghar Solar page.

How does VGK Care (insurance) work? +

VGK Care is an insurance facilitation service — it connects customers with established insurance providers across health, life, vehicle, and business categories. MyntReal acts as a facilitator, not an insurance issuer.

All insurance policies are issued directly by the respective IRDAI-regulated insurance companies. VGK Care assists with plan comparison, documentation, and claim guidance.

What is VGK Real Dreams? +

VGK Real Dreams is MyntReal's real estate facilitation vertical. It lists verified plots, apartments, villas, and agricultural land across Andhra Pradesh, and connects interested buyers with sellers and developers.

MyntReal acts as a facilitator in real estate transactions — not as a developer or direct seller. All transactions are documented and independently verified.
