MyntReal — Tirupati's EV Partner of Choice

From the Tirumala footpaths to the IT campuses of Renigunta Road, MyntReal's Manthra EV network is redefining mobility in the temple city.

Tirupati is one of the most extraordinary cities in Andhra Pradesh — a global pilgrimage destination receiving over 50,000 visitors per day to the Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams, while simultaneously emerging as a significant IT and educational hub in South India. The coexistence of a massive pilgrim economy with a growing IT corridor — anchored by SRM University, Infosys, and Tech Mahindra facilities along the Renigunta Road — creates two very distinct but equally powerful EV buyer segments in one city. MyntReal's Manthra EV programme is designed to serve both.

For the IT and education community in Tirupati, Manthra EV's LFP-battery scooters offer a daily commuting solution that matches the cost expectations and environmental sensibilities of a younger, salaried workforce. For the businesses serving the pilgrim economy — auto drivers, hotel staff transport, and last-mile mobility providers — fleet electrification creates measurable cost savings on high daily mileage. MyntReal's EV dealership in AP programme helps Tirupati entrepreneurs establish businesses catering to both segments.

APSRTC's active electrification programme, which includes Tirupati depot routes, is already sensitising the city's public to EV technology. This government momentum creates a supportive environment for private EV adoption. MyntReal's EV franchise partners in Tirupati benefit from this public EV awareness while targeting private two-wheeler buyers who are ready to make the switch from petrol.

Tirupati's EV Market: Faith, Technology, and Growth

Tirupati's growth trajectory over the past decade has been exceptional. The Tirupati International Airport has expanded with international connectivity. The Chennai-Bengaluru Industrial Corridor (CBIC) positions the Tirupati-Chittoor belt as a priority manufacturing zone. This industrial growth will create a large blue-collar commuter workforce — one of the primary user segments for affordable electric two-wheelers. At the same time, TTD's own electrification initiatives within the Tirumala complex have made Tirupati a showcase city for EVs in AP. Manthra EV's visible presence in this city carries brand value beyond just sales volume.

Why Tirupati's EV Market Is Unique in AP

No other city in Andhra Pradesh combines Tirupati's factors — the world's largest pilgrimage destination, a rapidly growing IT sector, an international airport, and proximity to the Chennai-Bengaluru industrial corridor.
