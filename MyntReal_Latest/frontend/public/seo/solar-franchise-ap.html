The Solar Franchise Opportunity in Andhra Pradesh

PM Surya Ghar is creating the largest solar installation wave in India's history — and AP is at the centre.

Combined with the Government of India's PM Surya Ghar (Muft Bijli Yojana) scheme offering up to ₹78,000 in central subsidy, the demand for quality solar installers in AP is at an all-time high.
