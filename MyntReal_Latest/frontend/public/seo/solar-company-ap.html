MyntReal — Andhra Pradesh's Trusted Solar Company

Affordable, subsidy-backed rooftop solar for homes and businesses — installed by certified technicians statewide.

MyntReal's Har Ghar Solar brand makes rooftop solar accessible to every home and business in Andhra Pradesh. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified multi-vertical company, MyntReal offers end-to-end solar services — from DISCOM application and MNRE-approved panel procurement to professional installation, net-metering setup, and PM Surya Ghar subsidy processing.

Andhra Pradesh receives some of India's highest solar irradiation — making rooftop solar one of the most financially viable investments for AP households. With electricity tariffs rising annually, a 3 kW Har Ghar Solar system can reduce electricity bills by 80–100% and generate ROI within 4–6 years. Under PM Surya Ghar, eligible households receive up to ₹78,000 in central subsidy — MyntReal handles this entire process for its customers.

For entrepreneurs, MyntReal offers a solar dealership in Andhra Pradesh and a structured solar franchise programme — complete with installation training, DISCOM liaison support, and access to MyntReal's customer funnel statewide.

Har Ghar Solar currently operates dedicated city solar teams across Visakhapatnam (AP's largest coastal solar market), Srikakulam (cyclone-resistant coastal and agri-pump solar), Vizianagaram (college, hostel, and NH 16 commercial solar), Vijayawada (AP's commercial capital), Guntur (agricultural belt solar), Tirupati (pilgrimage and APIIC industrial solar), Kakinada (port and industrial solar), and Rajahmundry (Godavari delta solar).
