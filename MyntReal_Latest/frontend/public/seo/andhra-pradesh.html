Why MyntReal for Andhra Pradesh?

A home-grown AP enterprise with deep local market knowledge and proven business models.

Andhra Pradesh is emerging as one of South India's fastest-growing markets for electric vehicles and solar energy. With the state government's proactive EV policies, high two-wheeler penetration across cities and rural areas, and rising electricity tariffs across APSPDCL and APCPDCL zones, the demand for EVs and rooftop solar has never been stronger.

MyntReal, headquartered in Visakhapatnam, is uniquely positioned as a home-grown AP enterprise with deep local market knowledge, an established dealer network, and direct relationships with APSPDCL and APCPDCL for PM Surya Ghar subsidy processing. Our Manthra EV brand is engineered specifically for Indian roads and our Har Ghar Solar programme has helped hundreds of AP families cut their electricity bills by up to 90%.

Whether you are an individual looking to purchase an EV, a homeowner wanting rooftop solar, or an entrepreneur seeking a profitable EV or solar franchise in Andhra Pradesh, MyntReal has a tested, scalable programme built for the AP market — from Visakhapatnam on the coast to Tirupati in the south.
