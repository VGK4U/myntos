MyntReal — Andhra Pradesh's Trusted EV Company

From electric scooters to complete EV dealership programmes, MyntReal is powering Andhra Pradesh's shift to sustainable mobility.

MyntReal, headquartered in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, is one of the state's most structured and rapidly growing electric vehicle companies. Operating under the Manthra EV brand, MyntReal offers a complete ecosystem of electric two-wheelers — including LFP-battery e-bikes and scooters — backed by a network of authorised dealers, trained EV technicians, and a dedicated after-sales support system.

MyntReal is uniquely positioned to serve this demand — with a distribution network spanning Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada, Guntur, Rajahmundry, Kakinada, Tirupati, and Nellore. Whether you are a customer looking to buy an electric vehicle or an entrepreneur exploring an EV dealership in Andhra Pradesh, MyntReal offers the right programme for you.

Beyond vehicle sales, MyntReal's EV franchise programme in Andhra Pradesh and EV business opportunity have helped hundreds of local entrepreneurs launch profitable EV businesses with full company support, territory protection, and co-branded marketing.
