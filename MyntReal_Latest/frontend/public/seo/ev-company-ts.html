MyntReal — Telangana's Growing EV Company

EV vehicles, dealerships, and business partnerships for Telangana entrepreneurs and customers.

Telangana is one of India's most progressive states for electric mobility. With Hyderabad consistently ranking among India's top EV-adoption cities, a state EV policy offering significant incentives, and rising fuel prices pushing commuters towards electric alternatives, Telangana offers exceptional opportunity for both EV buyers and EV business entrepreneurs.

MyntReal's Manthra EV brand is actively expanding its dealer network across Telangana. Powered by LFP battery technology, Manthra EV scooters and e-bikes are designed for India's urban and semi-urban riding conditions — making them ideal for Hyderabad's dense city traffic, Warangal's growing commuter base, and the smaller cities of Karimnagar and Nizamabad where EV supply is still limited.

For Telangana entrepreneurs, MyntReal offers an EV franchise programme and dealer model with exclusive territory protection, co-branded marketing, product training at EVolution Training Centre, and access to MyntReal's growing multi-vertical customer base including solar and insurance.
