MyntReal — Vizianagaram's Trusted EV Partner

From the historic Vizianagaram Fort area to Bobbili's textile markets and Parvathipuram's tribal corridors, MyntReal is bringing clean mobility to all of Vizianagaram.

Vizianagaram — often called the 'City of Valour' — sits strategically between Visakhapatnam and Srikakulam on National Highway 16, serving as a vital commercial and educational hub for North Andhra Pradesh. The city hosts multiple degree colleges and professional institutions, creating a large student and young professional population that forms a natural early-adopter base for affordable electric two-wheelers. Vizianagaram's vibrant markets for textiles (particularly Bobbili-style products), agricultural produce, and FMCG goods generate significant daily mobility needs across the district. MyntReal's Manthra EV platform addresses all these segments with its reliable, affordable LFP-battery electric two-wheelers.

Vizianagaram's strategic position on NH 16 — India's busiest logistics corridor — gives the city excellent road connectivity and makes it an ideal distribution hub for MyntReal's EV dealer operations. Partners who establish EV dealerships in Andhra Pradesh anchored in Vizianagaram can efficiently service not just the city itself but also surrounding mandals in Bobbili, Salur, Gajapathinagaram, and the newly bifurcated Parvathipuram Manyam district. The NH 16 connectivity also ensures fast inventory replenishment, keeping dealer stock levels optimised.

The tribal regions adjoining Vizianagaram — including parts of the Parvathipuram Manyam district — have been targeted by AP's tribal welfare programmes for clean energy adoption. MyntReal's EV franchise programme presents a compelling opportunity for local entrepreneurs to participate in this government-backed clean mobility push while building a sustainable commercial EV business. The combination of urban student demand, trading community adoption, and tribal area electrification schemes makes Vizianagaram one of North AP's most strategically important EV markets.

Vizianagaram's EV Market: Heritage City, Modern Mobility

Vizianagaram district has approximately 1.5 million residents and a growing urban economy centred on trade, education, and agriculture. The city's two-wheeler market is dominated by petrol vehicles, but the economic pressure to switch is intensifying. Students commuting between hostels and colleges, traders visiting mandal markets, and daily-wage workers travelling to Vizianagaram's industrial units are all prime EV adopters — and the Manthra EV price point and running cost make a compelling case for switching. Vizianagaram's textile heritage also creates opportunities for MyntReal's fleet programmes targeting the handloom and powerloom industry's logistics needs. MyntReal's dealer partners in Vizianagaram are positioned to capture a broad customer base across these diverse segments.
