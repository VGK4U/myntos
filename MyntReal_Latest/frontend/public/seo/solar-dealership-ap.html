The Har Ghar Solar Dealership in Andhra Pradesh

Capture the PM Surya Ghar wave in AP with an authorised solar dealership.

The Government of India's PM Surya Ghar (Muft Bijli Yojana) scheme is creating a historic demand for solar installations across Andhra Pradesh. With central subsidies of up to ₹78,000 per household and a government target of 10 million rooftop installations, authorised solar dealers in AP are positioned to capture years of strong, subsidy-backed revenue.

MyntReal's Har Ghar Solar dealership in Andhra Pradesh gives you exclusive territory rights, PM Surya Ghar channel partner enrolment, DISCOM-approved installer status, and MyntReal's installation training and support infrastructure. As an authorised dealer, you handle the complete customer journey — site survey, DISCOM application, installation, and subsidy disbursal — with full MyntReal backend support at every step.

Unlike independent solar installers who must build their own brand from scratch, Har Ghar Solar dealers in AP operate under a nationally recognised brand with DISCOM approvals already in place — dramatically reducing your sales cycle and customer acquisition cost.
