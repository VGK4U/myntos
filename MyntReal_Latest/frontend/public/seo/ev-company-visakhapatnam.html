MyntReal — Visakhapatnam's Trusted EV Partner

From the Steel City's industrial corridors to Rushikonda's IT hubs, MyntReal is driving clean mobility across Vizag.

Visakhapatnam — often called Vizag — is Andhra Pradesh's most populous city and its industrial powerhouse. Home to RINL's Vizag Steel Plant, HPCL's petroleum refinery, and a growing IT corridor at Rushikonda, the city hosts a large daily commuter workforce whose transportation needs are rapidly being redefined by electric vehicles. MyntReal, headquartered just north of Vizag city at Pendurthi, is uniquely positioned to serve this growing EV demand with its Manthra EV range of LFP-battery electric two-wheelers.

The commuter population in Visakhapatnam is enormous — lakhs of workers travel daily between residential colonies and industrial zones like Gajuwaka, Malkapuram, and the Steel Plant township. Fuel costs in this commuting pattern run into thousands of rupees a month per household. Manthra EV two-wheelers offer a compelling alternative: zero petrol cost, minimal maintenance, and a charging cost of just a few rupees per day. EV dealerships in Andhra Pradesh anchored in Visakhapatnam can tap this massive latent demand.

Beyond the industrial workforce, Visakhapatnam's tourism sector — covering RK Beach, Araku Valley, Borra Caves, and the Submarine Museum — creates opportunities for electric vehicle rental and last-mile mobility businesses. MyntReal's EV franchise programme allows entrepreneurs to build multi-revenue EV businesses targeting both commuter and tourism segments. With the Visakhapatnam Smart City Mission actively promoting sustainable transport, regulatory tailwinds further accelerate EV adoption across the city.

Visakhapatnam's EV Market: Scale and Opportunity

Visakhapatnam is Andhra Pradesh's largest city with a population exceeding 2 million and a registered vehicle base among the highest in the state. The city's diverse economy — spanning port operations, steel manufacturing, oil refining, IT services, education, defence, and tourism — creates a correspondingly diverse EV buyer profile. Fleet operators serving the Steel Plant's employees, tourism taxi aggregators running Araku routes, and young IT professionals in Rushikonda's office parks represent distinct EV customer segments. MyntReal's Manthra EV range, with its rugged LFP battery pack rated for India's coastal climate, is engineered for exactly these conditions. The city's coastal humidity and salt air, which accelerate corrosion in conventional batteries, are managed effectively by the LFP chemistry's superior thermal stability.
