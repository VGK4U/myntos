The EV Franchise Opportunity in Andhra Pradesh

India's EV revolution is accelerating — and AP is one of its fastest-growing markets.

Andhra Pradesh's electric vehicle market is growing at over 30% annually, driven by central government EV subsidies, rising fuel prices, and a state government committed to clean mobility. For entrepreneurs, this creates a once-in-a-decade business window — and MyntReal's EV franchise in Andhra Pradesh is the fastest, lowest-risk way to capitalise on it.

As a MyntReal EV dealer or franchise partner in Andhra Pradesh, you gain exclusive rights to sell and service Manthra EV vehicles in your territory. The Manthra EV range — powered by LFP battery technology — is designed for India's climate and road conditions, making it a compelling choice for daily commuters, last-mile delivery operators, and rural mobility buyers across AP.

Beyond vehicle sales, MyntReal's multi-vertical model means your franchise can also offer solar solutions, insurance, and real estate services — creating multiple income streams from a single storefront and customer relationship. This cross-vertical model differentiates MyntReal franchises from single-product EV dealers.
