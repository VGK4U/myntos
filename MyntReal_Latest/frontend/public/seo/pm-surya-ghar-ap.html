How MyntReal Handles PM Surya Ghar in AP

From your first call to subsidy disbursal — MyntReal manages every step.

1

Site Assessment

MyntReal's team visits your AP property to assess rooftop suitability, shadow analysis, and optimal system size.

2

DISCOM Registration

We register your property with the AP DISCOM and submit the net-metering application — a mandatory requirement for PM Surya Ghar subsidy eligibility.

3

Subsidy Application

MyntReal submits your PM Surya Ghar subsidy application through the National Portal — ensuring your eligibility is verified and documented correctly.
