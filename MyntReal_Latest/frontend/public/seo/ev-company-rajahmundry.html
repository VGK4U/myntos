MyntReal — Rajahmundry's EV Growth Partner

From the Godavari ghats to the paddy fields of West Godavari, MyntReal is building the EV network for AP's cultural heartland.

Rajahmundry — officially renamed Rajamahendravaram — holds a special place in Andhra Pradesh's cultural and historical identity. The city of Alluri Sitarama Raju and the legendary Godavari river bridge, Rajahmundry is home to one of AP's most educated and culturally engaged populations. It is also a significant commercial centre — the Godavari delta's rice, sugarcane, and aquaculture economy flows through Rajahmundry's markets, and the city serves as a banking and commercial hub for both East and West Godavari districts. MyntReal's Manthra EV programme serves this market with LFP-battery electric two-wheelers suited to Rajahmundry's mix of urban streets and rural district roads.

The Rajahmundry–Kovvur conurbation — connected by the famous Godavari bridge — has a combined population of over half a million, with daily commuting across the river adding to the city's vehicle density. Agricultural workers and traders travelling from delta villages to Rajahmundry's markets represent a consistent demand pool for economical electric vehicles. MyntReal's EV dealership programme allows Rajahmundry entrepreneurs to target both the urban professional and the rural agricultural commuter markets effectively.

The Godavari Pushkaram festival, held every 12 years and drawing millions of pilgrims to the river ghats, alongside annual Maha Pushkaram observances, makes Rajahmundry a high-footfall cultural destination year-round. Religious tourism creates ancillary transport and logistics demand that EV fleet businesses can serve. MyntReal's EV franchise partners in Rajahmundry can build businesses that span daily commuter sales, agricultural fleet programmes, and cultural tourism fleet services.

Rajahmundry's EV Market: Cultural Prosperity and Agricultural Wealth

The Godavari delta is one of India's most agriculturally productive regions — a prosperity that reflects in Rajahmundry's retail and commercial sector. The city has a high density of educational institutions including Adikavi Nannaya University and numerous professional colleges. This academic community generates a substantial and consistent student EV buyer market. At the same time, the delta's agricultural wealth — principally rice, sugarcane, coconut, and aquaculture — means farm families have disposable income to invest in productive assets like electric two-wheelers. MyntReal's Manthra EV range is priced to appeal to exactly this aspirational, value-conscious buyer: good quality, reliable brand, government-policy-backed, and demonstrably cheaper to run than petrol.
